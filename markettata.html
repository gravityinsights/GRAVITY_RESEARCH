Knowledge Hub: Market Scenarios

Bull Case: The "Best Case" scenario. It assumes everything goes right—strong economic growth, high consumer demand, and peak operational efficiency.

Base Case: The "Most Likely" scenario. This is the grounded expectation based on current trends and planned institutional growth.

Bear Case: The "Worst Case" scenario. It accounts for potential risks like economic downturns, regulatory hurdles, or increased competition.
