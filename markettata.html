Industry Overview (Auto Sector India)

Strategic analysis of the Indian Automobile Industry with a primary focus on the Tata Motors Passenger Vehicle (PV) segment.

1. Over the last 3 years, Tata Motors has transformed from a laggard to a leader in the PV segment.

2. FY24 marked a turning point with the company achieving its highest-ever annual sales in the domestic market.

3. The 'New Forever' philosophy has successfully refreshed the portfolio (Nexon, Punch, Harrier, Safari).

4. Strategic demerger plans into Commercial (CV) and Passenger (PV) entities aim to unlock shareholder value.

5. EBITDA margins have seen consistent improvement, crossing the 6% mark in the PV business recently.

6. Market share in PVs climbed from 5% in FY20 to nearly 14% by FY24/25.

7. The company leads the EV race with over 150,000 EVs sold to date in India.

8. Massive capital expenditure is focused on a dedicated EV platform (Acti.ev) for future models.

9. Reduction in net automotive debt has been a key fiscal priority, nearing a "Zero Debt" goal.

10. Focus is now shifting toward premiumization and localized battery manufacturing for FY26-30.
